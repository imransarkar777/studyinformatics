What is Education ERP?

Education ERP software aids in the most efficient automation and streamlining of the institute's everyday academic and administrative processes. The main function of education management software is to centralize all institute data and aid decision-makers. Education ERP software aids in increasing the productivity of their institution by managing online admission, student attendance, online fee payment, timetable, communication, academic management, examination management, payroll, and online learning. The education ERP system also offers multiple customizable features that help institutes in addressing regular day to day operations.

Our ‘Study ERP’ Software

"Study Informatics" offers schools, colleges, polytechnic institutes, and universities the complete education management system "Study ERP" to automate their academic and administrative operational procedures. An all-in-one integrated system streamlines administrative processes from accounting to human resources as well as the entire student life cycle from enrollment to graduation.
