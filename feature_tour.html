Ultimate Education ERP Software for all kind of Institutes


Study Informatics ERP system has every feature you will ever need to run your institution efficiently.

Study Informatics ERP Software provides user-friendly dashboards with login access for teachers, non-teaching staff, students, parents, and management personnel of your institution. The various modules available in Education ERP facilitate all the processes of your institution, from the admission of new students to generating transfer certificates when students complete their studies.

Study Informatics ERP has modules to manage Timetable, Attendance, Examinations, Gradebooks, Campus News, Hostel, Library, Transportation, Calendar, Events, and many more. It has a fully-fledged Human Resource module to manage the payroll and employee pay slips. The Finance module helps you to plan and allot different fee structures to students. It is also an excellent collaboration tool using its Task, Discussion, Poll, Blog, and Videoconference plugins. There is an internal messaging system within Study Informatics, but you can also integrate it with external communication tools like email and texting.
