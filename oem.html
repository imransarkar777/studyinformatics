What is Study Informatics OEM Partnership Program

Study Informatics Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) Partners are organizations that embed Study Informatics into their product offerings and then sell the combined package to the edtech market. Our OEM partners leverage Study Informatics’s beautiful user interface design, feature-rich modules and engineering excellence to give their clients compelling Education management systems quickly and cost-effectively. We will guide you to successfully replicate the proven business model of Study Informatics in your geography.

Reach & sell to more customers by embedding Study Informatics in your solutions package

In today’s competitive environment, every institution needs innovative solutions that easily integrate. They need flexible and efficient software that increases the institutions’ agility, optimizes productivity and can turn data into working information capital — all while lowering their total cost of ownership. With over 40,000 institutions already running Study Informatics, we ensure a world class experience for every institution. This opens up a world of opportunities for our OEM partners, from one time eGov projects to stable business models.

Our Engineering + Your Marketing & Sales = Magic!

We work with many OEMs around the globe to design, develop and deliver innovative Education management software solutions with Study Informatics, that drives business value for your customers and gives you a competitive advantage. Be part of the Study Informatics OEM partner program today and forget about writing a single line of code or hiring someone for it. As an OEM partner, you have access to the latest updates of Study Informatics as we develop it, with your expertise in sales.

Partner Dashboard

Access to exclusive Partner Dashboard. Create new instances & configure your solutions directly from the partner dashboard. No technical knowledge required

You keep watch! (Our Code & Our Support, Your Terms & Your Pricing)

The OEM Plan puts you in control of the terms and price that you set for your product. Our involvement can be confidential if you wish. You stay in control while Study Informatics Student Information System powers your solution.

Market centric product engineering

With the modular architecture, powerful APIs and built for customization philosophy, Study Informatics is engineered to handle the complex requirements of edtech market.

24x7 support

With the OEM license, you are assured 24*7 support from the Study Informatics Pro team. When you have the right guidance and support from a team of experts, reaching the market for your products and services becomes all the more easy.

Commercial open source licens

The commercial open source license offered By Study Informatics Student Information System guarantees you the safety and protection that you require to launch your product in the market. As an OEM partner, you have the power to modify Study Informatics code as you retain your own IP

Auto upgrades

You will keep on getting the latest updates of Study Informatics as long as you are an active OEM partner

ROI guaranteed

Around 80% of our OEM partners achieved Return On Investment(ROI) during their first year of operation. Some of them generated million dollar incomes with assistance from Study Informatics.

OEM partners are further categorized based on their commitment to the program and their ability to support the unique needs of OEM customers
